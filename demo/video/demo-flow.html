NetraShield MVP

Wazuh-first SIEM optimization tool that reduces alert noise, groups related alerts, enriches with local IOCs, and protects response actions with approval controls.

Noise Reduction

Batch-aware triage

  • Calculates signal score, noise score, queue, suppression decision, and analyst priority.
  • Persists correlation groups with representative alert, alert IDs, first/last seen, and suppression reason.
  • Shows L1/L2 investigation steps and raw event evidence in the UI.

Local Enrichment

Lab-safe IOC matching

  • Local IOC watchlist avoids sending lab/client indicators to external providers.
  • Alert detail can enrich source IP, destination IP, host, user, and raw event indicators.
  • External providers remain configurable but are not required for the MVP demo.

Case + SOAR

Analyst lifecycle

  • Cases track owner, priority, SLA, escalation, timeline notes, close reason, and resolution summary.
  • n8n webhook dispatch is available for lab workflows.
  • Containment actions are held as pending approval and require admin approve/reject.

Admin

RBAC and governance

  • Admin can create users, activate/deactivate accounts, and assign roles.
  • Viewer remains read-only; analyst can operate cases and triage; admin controls users and approvals.
  • Audit logs capture login, user changes, settings changes, and automation decisions.
