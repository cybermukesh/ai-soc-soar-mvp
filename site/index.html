Judge Summary

What We Are Building

The product is an AI-assisted SOC/SOAR layer for analysts. It reads SIEM alerts, removes repeated noise, gives concise attack analysis, groups related events into incidents, and starts response actions through n8n or Shuffle after analyst approval.

Industry Pain

Why It Matters

SOC teams face alert overload, repeated false positives, limited analyst time, and expensive enterprise automation platforms. Smaller teams often have detection tools but not enough people or budget to convert alerts into prioritized response workflows.

Token And Cost Controls

Low-token Cheap Cloud Policy

Do not fine-tune in MVP

Use prompt engineering plus structured JSON before spending on model training.

Send compact context

Only normalized alert fields, severity, rule, asset, timeline, and minimal history go to the model.

Cache duplicate triage

Repeated alerts reuse prior verdicts unless evidence changes.

Escalate low confidence

Mark unclear alerts for analyst review instead of repeated model calls.

Use JSON-only output

Keep responses short, parseable, and audit-friendly.

Reserve stronger models

Use larger models only for demo-critical or high-severity summaries.
